    Bhaktapur Heart & Diabetic Center

    Bhaktapur Polyclinic and Diagnostic Centre, Bhaktapur, operated by Bhaktapur Heart & Diabetic Centre Pvt. Ltd. (BHDC), is a super-specialty health care centre that offers diagnostic, preventive and curative healthcare services in Bhaktapur. It was established with a motive “Healthy Care for Healthy Heart” in November, 2012 (2069-08-06 B.S). It began as an initiative of a first patient-oriented, socially responsible health care center all under one roof, making superior quality healthcare services convenient and accessible for the Bhaktapur community.
    With latest in technology and a network of qualified consultants and staff, BHDC Bhaktapur Polyclinic and Diagnostic Centre, is serving through three branches in Tulachhen-8 (behind Araniko Sabha Bhavan), Sallaghari, Katunje-1 (just opposite to 99 Ropani Chaur) and Suryabinyak, Pandubazar in Bhaktapur.
    Bhaktapur Heart & Diabetic Centre Pvt. Ltd. (BHDC), started with the purpose of providing the quality health service specially related with Heart, Hypertension (High Blood Pressure), Diabetes (High Blood Sugar) and Thyroid Problems, now have all specialist consultants regarding different health problems. The centre has state of the art health care facility offering a wide range of Pre-counseling, diagnostic, counseling, treatment as well as post-counseling services. The centre has special features like a daily counseling classes, online record keeping and home delivery of every needed medical service.
